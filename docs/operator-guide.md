# Operator guide — FSM

Portal: no dedicated self-service e-procurement portal was located --
procurement guidance and opportunities are published directly on the
Department of Finance and Administration's own website
(`dofa.gov.fm/services/procurement/`), regulated by the Public
Contracts Act (FSM Code Title 55 Chapter 4). Entity: Registrar of
Corporations (Title 36) charter or a State's own incorporation record
(if that State has exercised its Title 36 §206 opt-in) + FSM wage/
gross-revenue tax filing (Title 54 Chapter 1 §§131-132). Citizen-bidder
engagements also clear the Public Contracts Act's own eligibility AND
value-tiered preference-percentage gate before a filing can proceed.
Foreign-investor engagements additionally require an FSM Foreign
Investment Permit (Title 32 Chapter 2) or, for economic sectors outside
the three National Categories, a State Foreign Investment Permit.
Human-gated filing only.
